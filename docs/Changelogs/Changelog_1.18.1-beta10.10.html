Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.10

Wizards and Warlocks make their return/debut! Additionally added lanterns, tweaked Defy Death, and made some quality-of-life improvements to some crafting recipes.

Added

Added: Lanterns - Decorative colored versions for each spell class
Added: Wizards and Warlocks! New mobs that are able to cast spells based on their spell class. Wizards look like villagers, and are neutral, whereas Warlocks are evil Illagers who are hostile to villagers and players alike
        Note: They do not currently spawn naturally, but there are plans in the future!
Added: Chromatic Blend - New crafting item, made from mixing Chromatic Solution and any dye

Changed

Changed; Defy Death - Cooldown reduction gained from upgrades increased
Changed: Mana Potion Recipe - Now a brewing stand recipe instead of a crafting table one, but you can get up to 3 potions from one dust block as a result

Other

Fixed: The number of glass bottles should now be consistent across recipes, meaning you should no longer gain or lose more than you put in intially
