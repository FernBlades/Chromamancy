Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.1

Since Biomes o' Plenty removed their gems, all of their corresponding staves have been removed. A new spell has been created in the place of the Terrestrial staff however!

Added

Added: Chromatic Artifact - Made from all 9 Infused Gemstones, and used as a crafting ingredient for the Artifact Staff.
Added: Artifact Staff - New Chromatic Exclusive spell, same effect as previous BOP Terrestrial Staff
Added: Amethyst Staff - New Chromatic spell, deals [2/4/6/8/10] Chromatic damage, and costs [1/2/3/4/5] mana.

Changed

Changed: Lapis, Diamond, and Emerald Staves' crafting recipes now use Copper ingots instead of Iron.
Changed: Summoning Spells that drop items now also account for looting levels of your weapons.
Changed: Summon Creeper now drops items as well, but the mana cost is increased to 100/90/80/70/60, and cooldown increased to 60/50/40/30/20 seconds.

Removed

Removed: BOP Amber Staff
Removed: BOP Amethyst Staff
Removed: BOP Malachite Staff
Removed: BOP Peridot Staff
Removed: BOP Ruby Staff
Removed: BOP Sapphire Staff
Removed: BOP Tanzanite Staff
Removed: BOP Topaz Staff
Removed: BOP Terrestrial Staff

Other

Fixed: Updated Mod Descriptor file
Fixed: Altars now drop themselves when mined
