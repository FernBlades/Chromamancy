Changelog for Spellcraft beta7.3.0for1.7.10

Finally added NEI integration, added special wolves, and made the Feed spell a lot less broken.

Added

NEI Integration! See recipes for spells in game

Special Wolves:
        - Can take damage, but once they hit 1 health, they do not take damage.
        - When at 1 health, they will no longer attack or defend their owners.
        - Can be pet by owner by left clicking with an empty hand.
        - When owner is too far away, and the wolf is not sitting, they will attempt to teleport as usual. If there are no blocks for them to teleport to, they will instead start riding on the owners head until the owner lands.

Changed

Feed:
        Changed: Now requires actual food to be stored with the spell, up to 100 food bars and saturation.
        Changed: Now restores the same amount of food and saturation.
        Removed: No longer scales with Amberine level.
        Added: Now has a food efficiency stat, which will reduce the amount of food consumed to restore hunger.

Inferno:
        Changed: Lowered the burn duration from 5 seconds per level, to 1 second per level.
        Added: Upgrades now additionally increase burn duration by 2 seconds each.
