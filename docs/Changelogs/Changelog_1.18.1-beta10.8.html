Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.8

Advancements are finally added! Additionally has some small quality-of-life fixes, and some texture changes.

Added

Added: Advancements! 22 in total, with one of them being a hidden one. Will serve as a mini-tutorial, and to show some late-game goals
Added: More heart colors to support extremely high levels of health
Added: You can now disenchant spells in a similar manner to how Wizard Robes could be disenchanted. This will allow the use of lower level enchantments early without worrying about having to recreate them later

Changed

Changed: Iridesultimate Berry's color has been brightened to make it look whiter
Changed: Updated all Gem Block textures
Changed: Chromatic Cape's icon has been updated to include the rainbow trim
Changed: Mana Cauldrons now return glass bottles that are used in recipes
Changed: Any seeds item can now be used in the Chromatic Altar to recieve Chromatic Seeds
