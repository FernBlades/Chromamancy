Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.2-beta10.12


Added

Added: Dolphin's Grace - Allows the caster to swim faster, like the vanilla effect.
Added: Dolphin's Grace Ally


Changed

Changed: Gust - Now has new knockback behavior, and should now be more consistent!
Changed: Recall and Teleport:
        Added: Upgrades now decrease the channel time, 0.4 seconds per upgrade.
        Changed: Now charge their mana cost on starting to channel the spell, instead of when it completes.
Changed: Capture Mob:
        Changed: Can no longer be used to capture invulnerable mobs, to prevent accidentally despawning critical (mall) mobs!
        Changed: Now checks which side of the block the spell hit when releasing the stored mob, and places them accordingly
