Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.2-beta10.12.1

A handful of quick bug fixes for new features added in 10.12, and some quality of life updates for Rosasplash berries.

Changed

Fixed: Recall and Teleport: Now properly utilize Upgrades to allow for reduced channel times
Fixed: Dolphin's Grace Ally - Fixed the projectile's missing texture
Fixed: Rosasplash Berries will now longer show 'failed spell' effects on the caster if they are caught in the area of effect. They will still be unaffected as usual, but there will be no extra visuals/sounds
