Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.3

Added Heat and Chill, fixed Wither's effect, and tweaked some Verdant spells!

Added

Added: Heat - Melts and heats up certain blocks! See the table at the bottom of the spell page to see which ones.
Added: Chill - Freezes and coolsdown certain blocks! See the table at the bottom of the spell page to see which ones

Changed

Changed: Wither - Increased damage dealt at levels 4 and 5 to counteract the invulnerability frames
Changed: Locate Ores - Decreased range by 4 blocks at all levels, reduced class bonus to 4 blocks, and decreased cooldown to 4 seconds
Changed: Touch of Life - No longer single level spell, increased mana cost to [200/175/150/125/100], and removed cooldown. All bonuses now affect cost instead of cooldown duration.

Other

Fixed: Server crash on Capture Mob, Disable Mob, Teleport when interacting with untamed tamable animals
Fixed: Capture Mob - Should now show the name of the captured mob correctly
