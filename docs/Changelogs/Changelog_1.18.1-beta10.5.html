Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.5

Several bug fixes with spell projectiles, and some quality of life changes to Teleport and Recall!

Changed

Changed: Thunder - No longer has a cooldown, and the lightning created by this spell is now only visible/audible to the caster, unless it is thunderstoming in the world. Cooldown has also been removed as a result.
Changed: Teleport and Recall - Volume of teleport sound reduced, and prevented playing the sound twice in some cases
Changed: Flight - Mana HUD now always displays while spell is active
Changed: Disarm - No longer has a cooldown, but mana cost increased to 125. Attack Berry and Upgrades now affect cost.

Other

Fixed: All direct damage spells should now effect the Ender Dragon. Unfortunately due to in-game immunities, damage over time effects such as Wither will still have no effect.
Fixed: Spell projectiles no longer save with the world, fixing a rare crash where a projectile would load with invalid data
Fixed: Spell projectiles no longer go through portals, preventing crashes when doing so
Fixed: Wizard robes should now change their sleeve size based on your skin (Alex or Steve model)
Fixed: Teleport and Recall - Fixed a bug where the spell would not properly go on cooldown when interrupted by damage
