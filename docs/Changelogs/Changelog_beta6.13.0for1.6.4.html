Changelog for Spellcraft beta6.13.0for1.6.4

This update brings massive changes to Wizard robes, and many bugfixes. Also in this update, many spells have had their damage converted to magic damage and tied to a specific color.

Added

New Armor Enchantments:
        There are now 9 new armor enchantments that can be added to any of the wizard robes.
        They provide 4% protection from a given spell class, including universal spells
        They also cause the robes to take more damage when hit with the given spell.
        Unlike other armor enchantments, they CANNOT be obtained with the Armor Enchantment spell.
        Instead, they have a normal crafting recipe.

Cloth blocks:
        There are now 9 new blocks of magical cloth, used in crafting and for decoration.

Changed

Strength:
        Changed: Now causes your punches to deal Vermillion magic damage.

Poison:
        Changed: Now deals Shadow magic damage.

Wither:
        Changed: Now deals Shadow magic damage.

Magic Dagger:
        Changed: Damage reduced to 2/4/6/8/10 from 6/8/10/12/14.
        Added: Magic Dagger now ignores all damage resistance from robes.
        Note: Does NOT deal Crimson magic damage.

Thunder:
        Removed: No longer sets blocks on fire, and no longer sets enemies on fire.
        Changed: Damage increased to 2/4/6/8/10 from 1/2/3/4/5.
        Fixed: Thunder will no longer be heard server-wide.

Explosion:
        Changed: Now deals Crimson magic damage to both the caster and any targets hit.

Incinerate:
        Changed: Lava now deals Verdant magic damage to target.

Nature's Wrath:
        Changed: Shockwave now deals Verdant magic damage to target.

Deplete Mana:
        Fixed: Now depletes the correct amount of mana from the target.

Summoned Mobs:
        Fixed: No longer show death messages when killed.
        Changed: Now deal Iridescent magical damage instead of physical, explosive and projectile.

Gem Staves (non-class specifc, Universal staves):
        Changed: Damage increased from 1/2/3/4/5 to 2/4/6/8/10.

Gem Staves (class specific):
        Changed: Now deal magic damage of their same color.

Wizard Robes:
        Changed: Robes will now work like gem tools, when taking damage they will try to use mana over durability.
        Added: Robes can now be repaired in an anvil using the matching magic cloth.
        Added: All Robes now provide 4% physical damage resistance.
        Added: Colored Robes now provide 4% magical damage resistance to their matching color.
        Added: Colored Robes now provide -4% magical damage resistance to their opposite color.
        Added: Universal Robes now provide 4% magical damage resistance to universal spells.

Gem Tools:
        Added: Tools can now be repaired using gems at an anvil.
        Fixed: HUD should now color correctly to show if user has enough mana.

Mana Boost Upgrade:
        Changed: Max upgrades lowered to 18 from 19. Maximum possible mana is now 9900.

Chalice of Harmony:
        Changed: Max stored mana potion increased to 9000 mB from 8000 mB.

IC2 Addon:
        Changed: Output of macerating a gem increased to 9 dust from 1.
        Changed: Compressor now requires a dust block to make a gem.

Damage Reduction Enchantment:
        Removed: No longer provides magical resistance.
        Added: Now provides 8% physical damage reduction per level.
