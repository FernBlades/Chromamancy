Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.2

Miscellaneous fixes, and added a few new spells!

Added

Added: Chromatic Solution recipe now drops a glass bottle, since otherwise it would be lost in the smelting
Added: Breed: Causes animals to fall in love
Added: Solar Flare: Deals damage and sets on fire, has double effect on undead

Changed

Changed: Level up milestone music is now quieter
Changed Defy Death: Duration increased to 30 seconds, and attack berry effect's duration boost increased to 15 seconds
Changed Capture Mob: Spell tome now shimmers when a mob is inside the book

Other

Fixed: Spells with differing XP rates have now all been applied.
