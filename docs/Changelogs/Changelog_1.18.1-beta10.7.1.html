Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.7.1

Mostly a bugfix update with mana bottles and Inferno, but also includes texture changes to Capture Mob and some XP rate changes to Heat and Chill

Changed

Changed: Natures Blessing - Additionally now adds a small increase to tiny dust drops when used on berries
Changed: Heat and Chill - Now have a higher XP rate to account for their high mana cost
Changed: Capture Mob - Now changes texture based on the mob captured within

Other

Fixed: Inferno - targets with fire resistance are now properly immune to the additional fire damage
Fixed: Magical Wolves no longer teleport to their owner's head when the owner is in Spectator mode
Fixed: Mana Bottles now correctly require 250mB of liquid mana to fill, instead of the previous 500
