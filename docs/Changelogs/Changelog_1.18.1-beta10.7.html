Changelog for Chromamancy 1.18.1-beta10.7

Major rework to the Nature's Blessing spell, changes to the mana cauldron recipes, new mastery capes, and many quality of life improvements for berry farmers!

Added

Added: Recipes to convert Dust back into Tiny Dust form
Added: Growth, Harvest, and Mutation fertilizers now have a 50% chance of not being used up when harvesting
Added: Fertilizer Blend - Mixture of Growth, Harvest, and Mutation fertilizers. Easy way to apply all 3 at once
Added: You can now dissolve Kunzite gems in the Chromatic mana cauldron
Added: Typed Mana Shards, one for each class. Currently only used as a crafting ingredient
Added: Mastery Capes, one for each class. Requires level 100 in all colors, but has a similar design to the base cape, just with an added chromatic trim!

Changed

Natures Blessing Spell effect completely changed, new effects:
        Removed: No longer grows berries by 1 stage
        Added: When used on Chromatic Farmland, small increase to growth rate, berry yield, and mutation chance to the next harvested berry, Additionally increases chance fertilizer is not used up
        Changed: No longer has a cooldown, but mana cost increased to 200/180/160/140/120
        Changed: Crafting recipe now uses Fertilizer Blend
Changed: Teleport and Recall - No longer teleport pets that are sitting
Changed: Summoned mobs no longer teleport to the caster, but Enthralled mobs still will
Changed: Heal Ally and Regeneration Ally - No longer deal the initial 0 damage hit, preventing certain mobs from becoming hostile
Changed: Berry crops can now be harvested by right clicking without sneaking
Changed: Slightly increased the effect of Mutation Fertilizer by 5%
Changed: Increased the effect of Harvest Fertilizer to 4
Changed: Mana Cauldron recipes no longer require a Chromatic Mana Cauldron as an ingredient. They instead use the new matching Mana Shards and gem blocks of the same color

Other

Fixed: Farmland that is watered will retain the watered state when converted to Chromatic Farmland
Fixed: Chromatic Farmland will no longer randomly become watered when no water is around
FIxed: Skillcapes are now the correct length to show the entire design
